Secure Ohio

Company that handles financial reporting for governing Ohio bodies have been noticing weak areas in their security and lack of cyber awareness. Currently working on securing their infrastructure and website to be less prone to SQL Injections, DDOS attacks as well as educating corresponding contacts the importance of having a secure domain and updated technology to handle today's modern era.

Password Encryption

Password encryption is a major part in securing digital assets. Password management software is available for purchase and most offer free versions. These password management systems allow you to store and archive all passwords a user utilizes to access websites. I personally use LastPass (not sponsored) to manage all my passwords using their encrypted password management vault to not have to worry about remembering all my unique passwords. The system also comes with a password recommendation with it's browser extension that generates a secure password. I believe all users whether it be an individual or a company utilize these systems in order to better secure and manage passwords to sensitive information
